Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

ClearPayoff does not collect personally identifiable information. All loan calculations are performed locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — displays ads to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization.
  • Microsoft Clarity — records anonymized session data to improve user experience.
  • RevenueCat — processes in-app purchases securely.
  • Firebase — stores app configuration data.

Data Storage

Saved loan scenarios are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

Free users see native ads served by Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. Pro users see no AdMob ads.

Children

ClearPayoff is not directed at children under 13.
