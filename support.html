Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Are the calculations accurate?

ClearPayoff uses standard amortization formulas used by US financial institutions. Results are estimates — always verify with your lender before making financial decisions.

How do I cancel Pro?

ClearPayoff Pro is a one-time purchase, not a subscription. There is nothing to cancel.

My calculation seems wrong

Email us at support@clearpayoff.app with your loan details and we'll verify the calculation.
